How RealText analyzes text

RealText uses algorithmic stylistic analysis — not a black-box AI model. It measures specific, interpretable characteristics of your writing:

Burstiness: Human writing naturally alternates between long and short sentences. AI tends to produce uniform sentence lengths. RealText measures this variance.

Lexical Diversity (TTR): The ratio of unique words to total words. AI text often reuses the same vocabulary, while humans draw from a richer word pool.

Repetition Patterns: AI frequently repeats structural patterns. RealText detects these n-gram repetitions across your text.

AI-Typical Connectors: Phrases like "It is important to note that" or "Moreover" appear at unusually high rates in AI text. RealText counts these per language.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. 5 free analyses per day without creating an account. No trial, no hidden costs, no credit card.

What AI models can RealText detect?

RealText detects stylistic patterns common to ChatGPT, GPT-5, Gemini, Claude, LLaMA, and Mistral. It analyzes writing style, not model-specific signatures.

How accurate is the detection?

Most reliable for unedited AI text. Less reliable for heavily edited content. We're transparent: the real value is in actionable suggestions, not just the score.

No. Your text is analyzed in memory and immediately discarded. We never save, log, or share submitted content.
