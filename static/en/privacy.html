Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

RealText ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and AI text analysis service at realtext.org.

Text Analysis Data

We do not store your text. When you submit text for analysis, it is processed in server memory and immediately discarded after the analysis is complete. We never save, log, or share the content you submit for analysis.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the service:

  • Anonymous usage counters: We track the number of analyses performed per IP address per day to enforce the daily free limit. No text content is stored.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs may contain IP addresses, browser type, and request timestamps. These are used solely for security and debugging purposes and are automatically rotated.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account creation or registration
  • We do not collect email addresses, names, or personal identifiers
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • We do not share any data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not store or log the text you submit for analysis

Third-Party Services

Our text analysis may use third-party AI APIs (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) to generate humanization suggestions. When this occurs, your text is sent to these services for processing only. These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We do not control how they process data beyond the scope of our API requests.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect our infrastructure, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), firewalled servers, and minimal data retention.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data or store user content, there is minimal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have questions about your data, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@realtext.org
